Kitaki is a platform which enables connection between Manufacturers, Dealers, and Wholesalers to their customers. Our tools and services allow exponential business expansion opportunity by enabling digitally with next gen mobile application platform. Our unique Business connect model enhances transactions through trusted contacts. Kitaki is member-only Business to Business market place.
